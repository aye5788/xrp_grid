SYNTHESIS FRAMEWORK:
Consider these signal combinations as you form your view:

Risk-ON signals (support longs, oppose shorts):
- Dollar weakening (DXY falling)
- Yields falling or stable
- Fear & Greed rising from fear territory
- BTC dominance stable or falling
- Positive and rising Coinbase premium
- Total market cap rising or stable

Risk-OFF signals (support shorts, oppose longs):
- Dollar strengthening (DXY rising)
- Yields rising sharply
- Fear & Greed falling or in extreme fear and deteriorating
- BTC dominance rising sharply
- Negative or falling Coinbase premium
- Total market cap falling

Mixed/neutral:
- Risk-on and risk-off signals roughly balanced
- Macro and crypto signals pointing in opposite directions

CONVICTION FRAMEWORK:
- HIGH: 4+ signals aligned in same direction, clear coherent macro narrative
- MEDIUM: 2-3 signals aligned, some contradictions but dominant lean is clear
- LOW: signals genuinely mixed, no clear directional lean, or key data missing
